SG

Secretary-General

Derya Satın

Dear Participants,

Greetings, I am Derya Satın and as the Secretary General of LB Model United Nations Conference 2026, it is an utmost honor for me to be welcoming you all for our first official session. I am a sophomore studying at Karşıyaka 15 Temmuz Şehitler Anatolian High School, also known as Karşıyaka Anatolian High School.

We, as the members of the executive team, have worked tirelessly day and night to provide you with the best MUN experience that you have or might experience. There were days when we stood up until late hours just to have you in our conference. I am very proud of our outcome and hope that you would have a great experience.

In MUNs you will participate in international conversations and debates in which you may need to work hard. We have prepared you differing committees with ranging agenda items for you to choose the one that you would like the most. You may sometimes feel miserable or sometimes very happy throughout the conference, and we will be there for you to endure those times. This is an opportunity for you to explore some skills which are deep inside you, form some friendships that may last a long time, and most importantly create memories. Therefore we invite you to build those memories with us on July 1-2-3 in LBMUN’26.

Moreover, we expect you to develop in not only academic ways but also in social ways. We want you to feel safe and free and form great relationships that will last you for a lot of time as well as having fun and enjoying the time you are at the conference with your friends. The youth is never coming back and I want you to enjoy it while you still can.

To conclude, we are coming up with one of the best conferences you might encounter in your conference career and as the members of the executive team, we would be more than happy and delighted to have you with us throughout our journey with LBMUN’26. So, expect the best and we will expect you in our conference.

Derya Satın

Secretary General of LBMUN’26

World Health Organization

WHO

After the industrial revolution and the isolation of humans, psychological and social problems have risen. This occurrence ultimately resulted in increased usage of psychoactive and harmful chemicals. The Group of 77 hopes to change this reality and find ways to offer mental health support to those in need. WHO is responsible for putting an end to this horrible situation.

Agenda Item: Achieving the ideal healthcare system in The Group of 77 (G77) to fight against the socioeconomic impacts, physical and psychological effects caused by abuse and addiction.
Beginner

UN Environment Programme

UNEP

Global warming affects not only humans, but animals throughout the past years rates of endangered species have gone devastatingly up. Scientists have managed to associate this with global warming. The mission of UNEP is to minimalize the effect of global warming on natural habitats, and to explore ways to protect endangered species.

Agenda Item: Addressing the Protection of the Environment and Endangered Species in the Face of Global Warming
Intermediate

Historical United Nations Committee on the Peaceful Uses of Outer Space

UNCOPUOS

In the ever-changing world after the industrial revolution, humankind has always sought to soar and explore the space. With a mission to create the necessary legal foundation, UNCOPUOS is aimed at creating the outer space treaty. These foundations play a crucial role in allowing organizations such as NASA to function.

Agenda Item: Creation of the Outer Space Treaty
Advanced

Historical Aerospace Industries Association

H-AIA

Established in 1919, the Aerospace Industries Association (AIA) stands as the premier trade organization representing the United States' aerospace and defense sectors. For over a century, AIA has served as the unified voice for the nation's leading manufacturers and suppliers, dedicated to fostering a robust, innovative, and globally dominant industrial base. Headquartered in Arlington, Virginia, the association acts as a vital nexus for industry collaboration.

Agenda Item: Open Agenda
Intermediate

Group of Twenty

G20

G20 is a financial gathering to promote transparency and trust between states. To accomplish global prosperity, this committee hopes to address some of the most crucial problems that the global economy is facing at this time.

Agenda Item: Open Agenda
Advanced

Historical North Atlantic Treaty Organization

H-NATO

The case of Saddam Hussein in Iraq became one of the most important international issues in 2003. Saddam Hussein was the leader of Iraq and was accused of developing weapons of mass destruction and supporting terrorism. Because of these claims, the United States and its allies started a military intervention in Iraq in March 2003. NATO countries had different opinions about the operation, and this created debates inside the alliance.

Agenda Item: In 2003, the crucial case of Saddam Husein in Iraq

Advanced

Joint Crisis Committee

JCC

A Crisis Committee in a Model United Nations (MUN) simulation, particularly one with a small group, is a fast-paced, highly interactive, and less formal committee that simulates intense, real-world emergency scenarios. Unlike General Assemblies (GA) that focus on long-term resolutions, a crisis committee requires immediate action to resolve rapidly changing, often unexpected, events.

Agenda Item: 1974 Cyprus Peace Operation
